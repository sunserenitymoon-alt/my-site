Mini plan (3–5 steps)

  1. Define your goal for “Time-block planner template” in one short sentence.
  2. Set a 25-minute timer and work only on this.
  3. Write a 3-line summary of what you did and what to do next.
  4. Save your notes (or printable) to a folder you can find again.
  5. Schedule the next 25-minute block on your calendar.

Quick example

Example: Time-block 25-min study + 5-min break ×4. Put math in the first block; languages after lunch when energy dips.
